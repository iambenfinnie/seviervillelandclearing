About This Area
Land Clearing in
Pigeon Forge, TN

Pigeon Forge sits at the gateway to the Smoky Mountains, and we clear land here every week — from vacation rental cabin lots to larger residential acreage. Whether you're a homeowner reclaiming overgrown property, an investor preparing a cabin build site, or a developer prepping a commercial lot, our team knows the terrain and gets it done efficiently.

The steep grades and rocky soil common around Pigeon Forge require equipment and experience built for mountain terrain. We use forestry mulchers and purpose-built clearing equipment that handles Sevier County's conditions without tearing up the land. Most residential lots are completed in a single day.

What We Do
Services Available
in Pigeon Forge, TN

Land Clearing

Full land clearing for residential lots, acreage, and commercial sites. We handle brush, trees, stumps, and debris — leaving a clean, graded surface ready for your next project.

Forestry Mulching

Single-pass mulching that grinds trees and brush into natural ground cover. No burning, no hauling. Faster and cleaner than traditional clearing — ideal for larger acreage.

Lot Clearing

Precision lot clearing for new home builds, cabin sites, and investment properties. We work around what you want to keep and remove what you don't.

Bush Hogging

Heavy-duty mowing for overgrown fields, pastures, and roadside lots. Keep your property maintained and fire-safe without the full clearing cost.

Site Preparation

Full site prep including clearing, grubbing, rough grading, and debris removal — everything a builder or homeowner needs before breaking ground.
