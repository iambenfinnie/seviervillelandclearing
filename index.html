§ 01 — Capabilities

What we do,
and what we don't.

We run tracked mulchers, skid steers with grapples, and dozers. We clear raw timber, undergrowth, brush, and brier — then leave the ground ready for a footer, a driveway, or a mountain view. We don't do landscaping.

01

Land Clearing

Full-site clearing on acreage up to 40+ acres. Trees, stumps, undergrowth — hauled or ground on-site, your call.

02

Forestry Mulching

Low-impact, one-pass mulching. No burn piles, no hauling fees. Leaves nutrient-rich mulch for erosion control.

03

Lot Clearing

Residential and cabin lots in the Smokies. Precise around setbacks, property lines, and protected trees.

04

Bush Hogging

Pasture reclamation, fence line clearing, right-of-way maintenance. By the hour or by the acre.

05

Site Preparation

Rough grading, pad prep, access roads, and driveway cuts. We work directly with your builder and surveyor.

06

Free Estimates

On-site walk, written quote within 48 hours. No pressure, no phantom fees, no bait-and-switch day-of.
